Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Thank you for using Gas Engineer Scheduler ("the App"). The App is developed and maintained to help individual gas engineers manage their bookings and schedules efficiently.

Your privacy is taken very seriously. The core principle of the App is that your data belongs exclusively to you. This Privacy Policy outlines what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information Collection and Storage

Gas Engineer Scheduler operates entirely offline. We (the developer) do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, customer lists, or scheduling data you enter into the App.

  • Local Storage: All data, including customer profiles, engineer details, and bookings, is stored locally in the App's database entirely on your device.
  • No Cloud Sync to Developer Servers: The App does not sync your data to any external developer-owned servers or third-party databases.

2. Cloud Backups and Device Sync

While the developer does not collect your data, your data may be backed up according to your iOS device settings:

  • If you have iCloud Drive or iCloud Backups enabled for your device or specifically for the App, your device will securely store automatic copies of the App's local database in your personal iCloud account. This data is exclusively accessible by you and Apple.
  • Manual backups exported from the application remain strictly under your control and on your specific device (or wherever you choose to save the exported files).

3. Information Use

Because no data is communicated to the developer, we cannot use your data for marketing, analytics, profiling, or sharing with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, tracking libraries, or analytics tools. However, Apple may automatically collect anonymous crash reports and basic usage metrics if you have explicitly chosen to "Share Analytics with App Developers" in your iOS system settings. These reports contain no personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is a professional tool designed for gas engineers and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.
