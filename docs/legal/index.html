Native screen capture and recording for macOS. No cloud. No account. Everything stays on your Mac.

Features

📸 Screenshots Full-screen, area, or window — captured instantly with global shortcuts.
🎬 Screen recording Record a visible screen, a custom region, or a single app window.
🫧 Camera bubble Optional floating circular camera overlay for demos and walkthroughs.
🎙️ Microphone recording Include your voice in recordings when you need to explain what's on screen.
📁 Local library Browse and manage all your captures directly on your Mac.
⌨️ Global shortcuts Trigger any capture mode without leaving your current app.
✏️ Watermark text Add a custom floating text watermark to branded recordings.
🎞️ GIF export Convert short recordings to animated GIFs for quick sharing.

Privacy

  • All captures are stored locally on your Mac — nothing is uploaded anywhere.
  • No account is required. The App Store version is fully unlocked at purchase.
  • No analytics, no crash reporters, and no third-party tracking SDKs are included.
  • Screen, microphone, and camera access are requested only when you use those features.
  • OrbCapture does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

FAQ

What macOS version do I need?

OrbCapture requires macOS 14 Sonoma or later.

Do I need to create an account?

No. The App Store version is fully unlocked after purchase — no sign-in required.

Where are my captures saved?

By default, captures are saved to a local app folder on your Mac. You can choose a custom save location in Settings.

Can I record with audio?

Yes. Enable microphone recording in the recording options before starting a recording session.

Is there a subscription?

No. OrbCapture is a one-time purchase on the Mac App Store.
