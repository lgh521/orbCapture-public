Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?
A: No. Gas Engineer Scheduler is built offline-first. All your bookings, customer records, and settings are saved securely on your device. You can completely manage your schedule without Wi-Fi or cellular data.
Q: How do I backup my data?
A: The app creates automatic local backups which are stored in your device's files. Additionally, if you have iCloud Drive turned on in your device settings, iOS will automatically encrypt and backup this data along with your standard device backups.
Q: Can I sync my schedule across my iPhone and iPad?
A: Currently, Gas Engineer Scheduler is focused on standalone, single-device offline reliability. We are actively exploring native cloud synchronization options for future updates.
Q: How do I generate a PDF report for a job?
A: Open any completed booking from the 'Bookings' tab or the 'Schedule' timeline. Tap the 'Share icon' or 'Generate PDF' button within the booking details view. The generated PDF will automatically include your Gas Safe number and configured company details.
