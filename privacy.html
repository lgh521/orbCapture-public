Privacy Policy

OrbCapture is built with a privacy-first approach. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • All captures are stored locally on your Mac — nothing is uploaded to any server.
  • No account is required. The App Store version is fully unlocked at purchase.
  • No analytics, no crash reporters, and no third-party tracking SDKs are included.
  • Screen, microphone, and camera access are requested only when you actively use those features.
  • OrbCapture does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
  • No data is shared with the developer or any third party.

Intended Use

OrbCapture is designed for personal learning, education, and legitimate content creation — including software tutorials, screen demonstrations, and sharing screen-recording or video-production techniques with others.

Typical lawful use cases include:

  • Recording personal screen activity for study or reference.
  • Creating tutorial or instructional videos for educational purposes.
  • Content creators sharing video production workflows and techniques.
  • Software demonstrations and walkthroughs for professional use.
  • Recording your own presentations or online meetings (with participant consent).
